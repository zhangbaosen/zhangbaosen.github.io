I joined the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Washington as an Assistant Professor in 2015. My research interests are in the area of power and energy systems, spanning from control/optimization, statistics and machine learning to power electronics. If you are interested in any of these, we can find things to chat about. My CV is here.

I completed my PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley in 2013, where I worked with David Tse and my thesis is here. I was a postdoc at Stanford, jointly hosted by Ramesh Johari and Ram Rajagopal. I received my undergraduate degree in Engineering Science (Electrical Option) from the University of Toronto.
