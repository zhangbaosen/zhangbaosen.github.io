CEI Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of Washington

Apr 11, 2024

The Clean Energy Institute at the University of Washington is now accepting applications for our CEI Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellowship. We invite innovative, exceptionally talented researchers who have recently received their Ph.D. degree (or foreign equivalent) to apply for full-time postdoctoral fellowship positions at the UW’s Seattle campus. The Fellowship is a full-time, 12-month position at the rank of Research Associate. The salary range is $75,000– $100,000 depending on the qualifications and scope of the research proposed by the applicant. Reappointment for an additional year is possible depending upon mutual agreement and funding availability. The Fellowship carries an annual discretionary research budget of $10,000.

Visit the UW CEI website to learn more, or apply directly via Interfolio. If you have any questions, please let me know.
