Outreach at the Seattle Science Center

Sep 23, 2018

Thanks to Chase, Ling, Tinu, Tanner and Victor for putting on a booth at the Seattle Science Center! We had demos of our research (joint with Lilian Ratliff), as well as props illustrating max-flow/min-cut and the Monty Hall problem. Surprising, kids got the Monty Hall faster than the adults!
